Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientists, and all the other scientific journals her worked had appered in, a well thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college and the longer she spend between these four walls the more she fell she needed to be free.

She had spend the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote procaliming that Work is the refugee of the people that have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work unravellling the secrets of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had something better to do.
